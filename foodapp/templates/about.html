Our Mission

HMF is dedicated to connecting home food cooks (FoodMakers) with food lovers (Eaters) who appreciate homemade meals. We believe in supporting home chefs, providing fresh, healthy, and affordable food options, and fostering a community that values home-cooked meals.

Authentic Home-Cooked Meals

Enjoy food prepared by passionate home cooks.

Support Local Food Makers

Help independent home chefs grow their small businesses.

Easy Ordering Process

Seamless food discovery, ordering, and delivery experience.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Eaters and FoodMakers create profiles to get started.

2. Discover & Order

Eaters browse homemade food options and place an order.

3. Food Preparation

FoodMakers prepare fresh meals with love and care.

4. Enjoy Your Meal

Receive your home-cooked food and savor the taste.
